Features

Everything a PE teacher needs

One platform from the starting gun to the trophy presentation. No spreadsheet juggling, no paper forms.

Live Carnival Timing

Record results heat by heat from any phone or tablet. Parents and staff see live updates the moment you hit Save.

Results & Records

Every result is stored and searchable. School records update automatically. Export to PDF in seconds.

House Points New

Points awarded automatically per place (8–6–5–4–3–2–1). Live standings visible to all on the day.

District Qualifiers

Automatic qualifier tracking based on your district cutoff standards. No manual cross-referencing.

Event Program Builder New

Build your carnival program before the day. The Next Event button auto-fills age group, gender and event — keeping things moving.

School & District Pages

Public-facing pages for your school, district, division and region. Share results with parents and other schools instantly.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No IT department required. If you can use a smartphone, you can run a carnival with Portal.

Contact us

Email or fill out the form below. We'll set up your school's account — usually within 24 hours.

Configure your carnival

Enter your houses, build your event program and set your district qualifier standards.

Run your carnival

Record results heat by heat from your phone. Live updates appear instantly for parents and staff.

Share the results
